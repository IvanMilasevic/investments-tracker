## Investment Thesis

Azure is growing at 40%+ YoY and is structurally positioned to take share
from on-prem IT budgets for the next decade. Copilot ($30/user/mo add-on)
is beginning to monetise the AI layer on top of Office 365, creating a
second growth engine with near-zero marginal cost. Microsoft has pricing
power, switching costs, and a balance sheet with ~$80B net cash — it can
buy back its way through macro weakness.

What must be true in 5 years: Azure reaches 30%+ cloud market share AND
Copilot/AI reaches $10B+ annual run-rate contribution. If either fails,
the premium multiple cannot be sustained.
